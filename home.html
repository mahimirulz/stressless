About Our Company

StressLess Hotline allows people who are breaking down from stress to talk to others going through similar situations. Statistics show that the average teenager suffers the same amount of stress as a working adult. StressLess opens doors that lead to support groups, licenced therapists, and a hotline for more urgent concerns. Our website is easy to access, and very organized. Our innovative program helps reduce irritation, anxiety, FOMO, loneliness.These are all factors that contribute to low self esteem issues.
